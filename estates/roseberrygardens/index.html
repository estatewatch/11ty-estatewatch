Roseberry Gardens estate, Sutton

Circa 150 homes have been earmarked for demolition on Sutton's Roseberry Gardens estate.

The Roseberry Gardens estate is one of seven estates listed in Sutton's Local Plan (2018) for potential redevelopment:

The estate is also clearly marked in Sutton's Policy Map as a potential renewal area:

The estate comprises around a dozen blocks of 2-3 storey maisonettes and around a dozen rows of 2-storey terraced homes. It is located directly adjacent to the Benhill estate which is a much larger estate, and also earmarked for redevelopment in the local plan.
